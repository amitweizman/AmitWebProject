About My Data

Most succesful apps on Google Play

We all use apps on a daily basis, and I personley very interested in them, so I decided to do this project about the most succesful apps on Google Play, the Android version of App Store.

My data base contains lots of useful information about these apps, such as their category, their rating, type, price and more. The subject is so interesting for me because I want to learn some day how to make apps and I want to try and learn what makes them succesful and help other app developers.
